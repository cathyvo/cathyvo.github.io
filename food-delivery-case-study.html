Project Overview

This study evaluated the usability of three major food delivery platforms (GrubHub, DoorDash, and UberEats) through a comprehensive between-subjects design. The research focused on user performance, task difficulty, and satisfaction to understand how intuitive each platform is for completing common ordering tasks.

Study Design

Methodology:

Between-Subjects Study with 63 total participants (21 per platform) to ensure unbiased comparison across platforms.

Standardized Tasks:

1

Order a Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Navigate to Chipotle, select a burrito bowl, and proceed to a set delivery address.

2

Adjust Pickup Time

Modify the scheduled pickup time by adding 30 minutes to the original time.

3

Modify Order Details

Modify the order details, then proceed to checkout.
