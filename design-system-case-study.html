The Challenge

As the product grew, inconsistencies in design and development became apparent. Different teams were creating similar components with slight variations, leading to:

  • Inconsistent user experience across the platform
  • Increased development time due to recreating similar components
  • Difficulty maintaining brand consistency
  • Onboarding challenges for new team members
  • Lack of clear design guidelines and standards
